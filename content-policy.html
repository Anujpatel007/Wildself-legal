WildSelf — Content & Safety Policy

Last updated: July 2026

WildSelf, operated by Anuj Patel, is built for creative, consensual self-expression. To keep everyone safe, the following content is strictly prohibited and is blocked or removed.

Strictly Prohibited

  • Nudity, sexual, or intimate imagery of any real, identifiable person.
  • Any content involving minors.
  • Non-consensual imagery — transforming a real person's photo without their permission.
  • Impersonation intended to deceive, defame, or harm.
  • Harassment, threats, hate speech, or incitement to violence.
  • Content that is illegal in your jurisdiction.

How We Enforce This

  • A consent confirmation is required before every generation.
  • Requests containing prohibited terms are automatically rejected.
  • AI prompts include safeguards to keep output tasteful and safe-for-work.
  • Accounts that abuse the service may be terminated.

Consent Comes First

Only upload photos of yourself or photos you have explicit permission to use. Creating explicit or deceptive imagery of a real person is harmful and, in many places, illegal — regardless of your age.

Report Abuse
