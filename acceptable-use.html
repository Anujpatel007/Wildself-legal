WildSelf — Acceptable Use

Last updated: July 2026

What WildSelf Is For

Transforming your own selfies (or photos you have permission to use) into stylized, artistic, glamorous, and fantasy avatars for personal, non-deceptive use.

Do

  • Upload clear photos of yourself.
  • Explore bold, artistic, and fantasy styles.
  • Share your creations, clearly as AI-generated art.

Don't

  • Upload anyone's photo without their consent.
  • Attempt to create nude/sexual content of a real person.
  • Pass off AI images as real photos to deceive others.
  • Target, harass, or embarrass any individual.

Breaking these rules can lead to account removal. When in doubt, don't upload someone else's face.
