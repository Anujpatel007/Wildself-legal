WildSelf — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

WildSelf (the "app") is operated by Anuj Patel ("we", "us"). We respect your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. For any question or data request, contact us at patelanuj136@gmail.com.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account data: your email address and (optionally) your name.
  • Content you create: the photos you upload for stylization and the avatars we generate for you.
  • Basic technical data needed to operate and secure the service.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To create your account and let you sign in.
  • To generate stylized avatars from photos you upload.
  • To store your generated avatars in your private gallery so you can access them later.

We do not sell your personal data.

3. Photo Processing

Photos you upload are sent to our AI image provider solely to generate your requested avatar. You should only upload photos of yourself, or photos you have explicit permission to use. Creating sexual, nude, or deceptive imagery of any real, identifiable person is strictly prohibited.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

Your avatars remain in your gallery until you delete them. Within the app you can delete individual avatars, clear your entire gallery, or permanently delete your entire account and all associated data at any time from the Profile screen. To request deletion by email, contact patelanuj136@gmail.com.

5. Children

This app is intended for adults 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

6. Security

Passwords are stored hashed. Access tokens are stored securely on your device.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the latest revision.
